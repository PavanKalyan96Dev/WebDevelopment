AP IIIT RGUKT RK Valley (Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies) is a premier educational institution located in the serene and picturesque region of Idupulapaya, Andhra Pradesh. It was established to provide quality technical education to rural youth and is renowned for its integrated six-year B.Tech program, designed to nurture students from their high school level through undergraduate studies. The institution offers world-class infrastructure, state-of-the-art labs, and a robust curriculum aimed at fostering innovation and research. With a strong focus on holistic development, AP IIIT RGUKT RK Valley is committed to producing skilled engineers and professionals who can contribute to the technological advancement of the nation.
